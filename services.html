How we work together

Two simple engagement models, designed to match the kind of problem you're solving.

Engagement · 01

Project-based

Fixed scope, fixed outcome. Best for clear problems with a defined deliverable: a strategy document, a product redesign, a launch-ready feature. We agree on scope, timeline, and price upfront, then deliver.

Engagement · 02

Sprint

One to two week intensive engagements. Best when you need to move fast: validate a concept, ship a prototype, or unblock a strategic decision. Small focused team, daily progress, real output by the end of the sprint.
